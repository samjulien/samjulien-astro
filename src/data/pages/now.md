I own our developer and IT admin experience across our AI Studio product, including third-party model integrations, guardrails, observability, the public API, SDKs, [developer docs](https://dev.writer.com/), and framework integrations like LangChain and Strands Agents. I'm also PM'ing two upcoming features — one aimed at vibe coders and one that will be a new full code experience. It's an intense, fast-paced job but incredibly interesting and engaging. I honestly can't believe how lucky I am to have a front-row seat to the AI revolution.
